About Us

American BioSources Inc. USA (ABS) originally founded in 2001 by two world renowned senior scientists based on the strong research foundation for innovation, novelty, and intellectual property generation for unmet medical needs. Our founding directors have business and technology management experience from global Biopharma public traded companies with proven track record.
